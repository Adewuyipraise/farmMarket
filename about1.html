About Us

At Farm Market we aim to provide a platform where farmers can sell their products directly to the consumers in a cheaper and better way.

Our Vision

To enable local Farmers to sell their products directly and profitably to consumers, Who gets fresh farm produce cheaper by digitizing the middleman.

Our Goal

To empower more farmers and digitize the middle man role so as to make food products cheaper.

Our Mission

To create a Technology Solution that will enable farmers to sell profitably and consumers to buy cheaply.
